About Chancy

Over the last 10 years I've worked as a product manager, project management support, and business process/tool owner in the telecommunications industry. After making the difficult decision to leave a large telecom, I decided to take on a new challenge of learning fullstack web development (which I recently recieved my certification for). I also have my Project Management certification and am a Certified Usability Analyst (which, if you think about it, makes me triple threat!).

On a personal note, I grew up on a 1000 acre ranch in northwestern Wyoming, making me a country boy at heart. Now find myself owning a 10 acre mini ranch of my own (after many years of trying to escape country living, I longed for it!). I joke that in my spare time that I'm going to start a mini cattle ranch (you know miniature cows, and wrangle them using a miniature horse and my 8 pound Morkie, named Charlemagne) but for now its just nice to have some wide open space.

Besides wanting to run a mini cattle ranch, I also enjoy camping, 4 wheeling, hiking, cooking, and reading (and drinking pina coladas, long walks on the beach...oh wait...).
